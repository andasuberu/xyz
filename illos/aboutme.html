About Me

Hi! I'm Anda Suberu, a multidisciplinary visual designer and illustrator based in Abuja, Nigeria.

I work with various people to create custom vector illustrations and assets that enhance brand identities, digital experiences, and print. My style is minimalist and I like to use clean lines, bold shapes and vibrant colors.

I mainly work with vector-based tools and I am open to various forms of collaboration.
