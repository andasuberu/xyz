About

I’m Anda — a multidisciplinary visual designer.
I enjoy designing to solve real problems and working with people who are as excited to bring their vision to life as I am.

My work blends technical precision from my background in computer engineering with creativity from traditional art. I enjoy making designs and building websites that are both functional and visually engaging, and illustrating concepts that bring a clear idea to life.

I’ve worked across a mix of projects, from branding and illustration to product interfaces and websites. And over time I’ve built for small businesses, startups, and personal creative ventures.

I always aim to make my designs approachable, impactful, and results-driven. In my downtime, you'll probably find me watching a good TV show, playing Mini Motorways, learning Japanese, or hitting the gym.
