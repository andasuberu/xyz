About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary visual designer and illustrator helping startups and global brands create clear and intentional design.

Focused on making things that work and feel right, I've handled a wide range of projects, from branding and illustration to product interfaces and websites. And over time I’ve built for startups, small and large businesses, and personal creative ventures.

My work blends technical precision from my background in computer engineering with creativity from traditional art. I love making designs and building websites that are both functional and visually engaging, and illustrating concepts that bring a clear idea to life.

I always aim to make my designs approachable, impactful, and results-driven. When I'm not working, you'll probably find me watching a good TV show, playing Mini Motorways, or learning Japanese.
